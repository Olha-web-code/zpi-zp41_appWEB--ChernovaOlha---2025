Topic: Creating a Free Website for Learning IT Technologies "WowDev"

Project Goal

To create a free educational platform for beginner developers, allowing users to gain basic knowledge about IT, get acquainted with modern technologies, develop skills through interactive materials and exercises, and join a community of IT newcomers.

Website Usage Scenario

1) Exploring Courses and Materials

The user visits the site and browses available free courses in areas such as programming basics, web development, databases, working with developer tools, and more. Each course includes a description, training program, and basic skills required to complete the course.

2) Registration and Profile Creation

The user can register on the platform and create a profile, specifying their interests, technologies they want to master, and learning goals to receive personalized recommendations.

3) Accessing Learning Materials

The user has access to lessons in text format, interactive exercises, and video materials. Progress is automatically saved, and the user sees the percentage of course completion and receives additional recommendations.

4) Completing Practical Tasks

Users complete exercises and practical tasks to reinforce what they have learned, receiving hints and feedback.

5) Community and Discussions

Users can communicate with other beginners, ask questions, discuss materials, share successes, and support each other. A forum or chat is provided for discussing general IT topics, solving problems together, and sharing experiences.

6) Receiving Recommendations

Based on activity and completed courses, the user is offered new courses and materials that match their interests.
